About

Dr. Ioannis Kontopoulos is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Informatics and Telematics of Harokopio University of Athens. He received his BSc and PhD from the same University in 2016 and 2022 respectively. Since then, he is working under the supervision of Associate Professor Kostantinos Tserpes. His doctoral research lies upon the area of Spatiotemporal Analysis and specifically in the "Distributed and Real-time Trajectory Classification and Anomaly Detection on Mobility Data". His research interests revolve around Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Distributed and Real-time Processing, Trajectory Analysis and Mobility Data. He has participated in several EU- and Nationally-funded projects tackling research challenges regarding scalability, event detection, machine learning and artificial intelligence.
